The site of ITB today


ITB has some of it's relic hedges to this day and remains as a green campus.
The hedgerows in Fingal County account for 1200ha, of “linear woodland” cover. There is a drive to increase the woodland coverage in Fingal to 7650ha by 2030 (Fingal County Council, 2010).
