2018 - The college today

ITB has expanded through the years and has introduced many new courses like Creative Digital Media for example. As of today, ITB is joining with three other Institutes of Technology in Dublin, DIT and ITT. Together they will for the Technological University of Dublin and from 2019, students will graduate from a University and not an Institute.
